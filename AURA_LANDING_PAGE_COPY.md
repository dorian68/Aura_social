## How it works (3 steps)

**Eyebrow:** The journey

**Title:** From audience to owned community in three steps.

**Step 1:** Launch your Superfan Club
> Create your club page in 5 minutes. Fans sign up, earn points and see their status on the leaderboard — free, forever.

**Step 2:** Activate your fans with challenges and rewards
> Set challenges (post a photo, visit a partner, share a story). Fans earn points. Top fans earn rewards — digital or physical, from you or a local sponsor.

**Step 3:** Turn engagement into revenue
> See who your real fans are on your dashboard. Generate sponsor-ready reports. Run partner campaigns with QR attribution. Know exactly which fans visited which business.
