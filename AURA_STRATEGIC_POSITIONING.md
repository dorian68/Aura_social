**For** mid-tier creators (10K–500K followers) who want to turn their engaged followers into a real community,  
**Aura** is a loyalty and rewards OS that identifies superfans, activates them with points and challenges, and connects them to partner businesses for measurable revenue —

## What Aura is (v2)

Aura is a **Creator Superfan OS** — an operating system for managing, activating and monetizing a creator's true fan community.

The core product is a **Superfan Club**: a branded fan club page the creator launches in minutes, where fans sign up, earn points, complete challenges, climb leaderboards and redeem rewards.

The business layer is a **partner campaign engine** that connects the creator's activated community to local businesses or brands who want measurable traffic, not just impressions.

## The strategic wedge

**Free Superfan Club for creators.**

Every creator can launch a Superfan Club at zero cost. No card, no setup friction, 5-minute launch. This creates:

1. A creator-owned community graph (fan emails, preferences, engagement history).
2. Points ledger data that proves engagement quality (which fans are real).
3. A platform for partner campaigns once the community is active.

The free tier is not a loss leader — it is the data and community asset that makes everything else valuable.

## Target creator profile (v2)

### Primary: Mid-tier, community-led creator

- 10K–300K followers (any platform)
- Has a loyal core audience that comments, saves, shares and DMs
- Has tried or is thinking about: Patreon, Substack, merch, Beehiiv
- Hasn't found a tool that activates the fans they already have
- Located in a city with local partner business potential

### Secondary: Local city-embedded creator

- Often lifestyle, food, fitness, beauty, fashion niche
- Audience is geographically concentrated (same city/region)
- High potential for local business partnerships
- Needs to prove community value to pitch local sponsors

### Not primary (for now):

- Macro creators (500K+): already have agencies, tools, teams
- Pure content play without community intent
- Creators without any engagement (reach without connection)

## Go-to-market v1 (pilot phase)

### Phase 1: Manual pilot (now → Q3 2026)

- Find 10–20 creator pilots (50K–200K followers, local audience, any platform)
- Launch their Superfan Club manually with white-glove onboarding
- Find 3–5 local partner businesses per creator city
- Run first campaigns manually (no full automation)
- Measure: fan signups, points earned, challenge completions, QR redemptions

**Success metric:** ≥3 creators with ≥100 fan signups, ≥1 real partner campaign with measurable traffic.

### Phase 2: Self-serve Superfan Club (Q3–Q4 2026)

- Creator can launch their club page without assistance
- Fan signup, points, challenges, leaderboard, rewards all self-serve
- Creator dashboard shows fan activity in real-time

### Phase 3: Partner marketplace (Q4 2026+)

- Partner businesses can discover creators by city + community size
- Campaign creation and QR/coupon tracking automated
- Platform commission revenue kicks in

## What we're NOT selling right now

The following are real parts of the product but should not lead the pitch until community activation is proven:

- Blockchain / token infrastructure
- NFT or fan pass minting
- B2B agent automation
- AI-powered campaign generation
- Stripe payment automation
- On-chain loyalty state

These features exist in the codebase. They activate only after a creator has 100+ real fan signups.
