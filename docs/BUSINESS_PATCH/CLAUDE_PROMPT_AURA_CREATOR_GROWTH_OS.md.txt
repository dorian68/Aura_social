# Prompt for Claude/Codex — Update Aura Into Cross-Platform Creator Superfan OS

## Role

You are a senior product engineer, UX strategist and business-aware builder.

Your task is to update Aura’s product direction, functional specs and landing page so that Aura becomes:

A cross-platform loyalty and rewards OS for creators who want to turn engagement into revenue.

Core positioning:

Aura helps creators turn their most engaged fans into a rewarded growth community.

Business version:

Aura is a loyalty and rewards OS for creators who want to turn engagement into revenue.

Local version:

Aura helps local creators transform their superfans into measurable traffic for partner businesses.

Hero line:

Your next 100 superfans are worth more than 100,000 passive followers.

Immediate explanation:

Aura gives those 100 superfans a reason to engage, share, show up, buy and bring others.

## 1. Strategic update

Aura must no longer be positioned as only an Instagram monetization platform.

Aura must become cross-platform by design:

- Instagram
- TikTok
- YouTube
- Twitch
- Discord
- Newsletter
- WhatsApp
- Events
- Offline rewards

However:

MVP = Instagram-first  
Architecture = cross-platform  
Positioning = creator community ownership layer

## 2. Product pivot

The immediate product wedge is:

Free Superfan Club for creators.

Aura should let a creator launch:

- a public fan club page;
- a points system;
- challenges;
- rewards;
- leaderboard;
- fan signup;
- fan referrals;
- creator dashboard;
- QR/coupon tracking.

The first creator outcome:

“I know who my real fans are, and I can activate them.”

The first fan outcome:

“My support counts, and I can earn status, points and rewards.”

The first Aura outcome:

Fans register on Aura and create a permissioned community graph.

## 4. Product philosophy requirements

Update the philosophy to state:

Aura exists to help creators own, activate and monetize their true fan communities.

Key principles:

- Creators first
- Fans get real value
- Points before tokens
- Cross-platform ownership
- Instagram-first but not Instagram-dependent
- Businesses enter after fan activation is proven
- Data is permissioned community intelligence, not raw data for sale

Update the landing page to sell to creators.

Lead with:

## 9. Technical acceptance criteria

The update is complete only if:

- The page no longer feels Instagram-only.
- The hero clearly sells Superfan Club value.
- Blockchain/token language is secondary or removed from the first screen.
- Creator can understand the value in under 10 seconds.
- The product supports creator club pages.
- Fan signup is possible.
- Points ledger exists or is specified.
- Challenges exist or are specified.
- Rewards exist or are specified.
- Creator dashboard exists or is specified.
- QR/coupon tracking exists or is specified.
- Cross-platform data model is present.
- Pilot CTA exists.

- Do I know what I get for free?
- Do I understand why my fans would join?
- Do I understand how I can make money later?
- Do I trust the product?
- Would I apply for a pilot?

Score:
