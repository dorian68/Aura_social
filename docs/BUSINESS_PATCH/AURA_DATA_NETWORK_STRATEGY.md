# Aura — Data & Network Strategy

## Strategic asset

Aura’s long-term value is the graph of activated creator communities.

The strategic asset is:

permissioned fan-community intelligence.

Not raw data.

## What Aura must capture

Aura should capture:

- fan identity;
- creator memberships;
- points history;
- challenge completions;
- reward redemptions;
- referrals;
- event attendance;
- QR scans;
- coupon usage;
- platform source;
- consent;
- local intent signals.

This enables Aura to understand:

- who is truly engaged;
- who brings others;
- who redeems offers;
- who attends events;
- who overlaps between communities;
- which creator communities are commercially activable.

## Do not sell raw fan data

Never position Aura as:

We sell fan data.

Position Aura as:

We help creators and partners activate consent-based fan segments.

The monetizable product is:

- campaign access;
- segment activation;
- sponsor reports;
- community intelligence.

## Fan consent

Every fan signup must include:

- terms acceptance;
- privacy consent;
- marketing opt-in optional;
- creator communication opt-in;
- partner reward opt-in optional.

Aura must respect:

- user trust;
- creator trust;
- GDPR principles;
- clear opt-out;
- data minimization.

## Future data products

Once enough communities exist, Aura can offer:

- audience overlap reports;
- collaboration recommendations;
- sponsor segment reports;
- local fan activation maps;
- top ambassador identification;
- cross-community affinity;
- event targeting;
- reward performance benchmarks.

## Audience overlap

One high-value insight:

Creator A and Creator B share 18% of their top engaged fans.

This enables:

- collaboration;
- joint event;
- co-sponsored campaign;
- shared drop;
- bundle reward;
- local tour.

## Platform value formula

Aura’s platform value grows with:

number of creators  
× activated fans per creator  
× repeat fan actions  
× cross-community overlap  
× measurable reward redemptions

Follower count alone is not enough.

## MVP data goal

For the first 3 pilots:

- 3 creators;
- 150–300 fans;
- at least 60 active fans;
- at least 30 challenge completions;
- at least 10 referrals;
- at least 10 reward redemptions.

## Strategic rule

Every feature should answer:

- Does this help a creator bring fans into Aura?
- Does this help a fan take action?
- Does this help Aura measure community value?
- Does this help future monetization?

If not, delay it.
