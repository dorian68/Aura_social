# Aura — Landing Page Rewrite Brief

## Goal

Update Aura’s public-facing page so that a creator immediately understands:

Aura helps me identify, reward and activate my real fans.

The page should convince a creator to launch a free Superfan Club.

The page should not primarily sell blockchain, tokens, AI agents or a marketplace.

## Hero

Headline:

Subheadline:

Aura helps creators turn their most engaged fans into a rewarded growth community — with points, challenges, rewards and fan data they own.

CTA:

Launch your free Superfan Club

Secondary CTA:

See how it works

## How it works

### 1. Launch your Superfan Club

Create a public page where fans can join, earn points and see rewards.

### 2. Activate your fans

Run challenges for referrals, events, QR scans, purchases, content support and community actions.

### 3. Turn engagement into revenue

Use your fan data to sell events, rewards, memberships or partner campaigns.

## Why followers are not enough

Followers are rented. Algorithms change. Reach disappears.

But your real fans are still there.

Aura helps you identify the people who repeatedly engage, invite others, show up and support your work — then gives them a reason to do more.

## FAQ

### Is this only for Instagram?

No. Aura is cross-platform. We start with Instagram because many creators already build engagement there, but Aura is designed for TikTok, YouTube, Discord, WhatsApp, events and offline rewards.

### Do I need crypto?

No. Aura starts with simple points and rewards. Fan passes and tokenized ownership can come later for creators who need it.

### What do my fans get?

They earn points, status, access and rewards for supporting your community.

### How do I make money?

You can use Aura to sell events, unlock rewards, create partner campaigns, build paid communities and prove your fan value to sponsors.

## Final landing page promise

Aura gives your real fans a reason to engage, share, show up, buy and bring others.
